Instructions For Registration & Admission Form

1. To fill registration form of your ward, go to Side Menu -> For Applicant -> Registration Form

2. To fill admission form of your ward, go to Side Menu -> For Applicant -> My Application -> Admission Form

Note : You can fill admission form of your ward if and only if the registration form is accepted by the school

3. Both registration and admission forms are single forms and will not allow to save in between. So you'll need to fill the complete form in order to submit your application

4. While filling your 2nd application from this account, in 'Details of Legal Guardian', select 'Existing Parent' and write your 'Parent ID' mentioned above in 'Parent ID' field. (If you want to use your previous details).

5. All the documents must be of size <= 1MB

6. Please be ready with all documents mentioned below to fill registration and admission form

7. On submitting the admission form, if any mandatory field remains unfilled, then you need to upload the all required documents again.

Documents Required

Registration Form

1. Child's Photo

2. Father's Photo (Optional)

3. Mother's Photo (Optional)

Admission Form

1. Father's Photo

2. Mother's Photo

3. Guardian's Photo (If Any)

4. Special Education Need (SEN) (If Any)

5. Father's Passport

6. Father's Emirates ID

7. Father's UAE Residency VISA (For other than UAE nationals only)

8. Mother's Passport

9. Mother's Emirates ID

10. Mother's UAE Residency VISA (For other than UAE nationals only)

11. Student's Passport

12. Student's Emirates ID

13. Student's UAE Residency VISA (For other than UAE nationals only)

14. Guardian's Passport (If Any)

15. Guardian's Emirates ID (If Any)

16. Guardian's UAE Residency VISA (For other than UAE nationals only) (If Any)

17. Birth Certificate

18. Vaccination Report

19. Family book for UAE nationals only
